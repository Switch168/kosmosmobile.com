Kosmos Free Call And Text

Kosmos lets you make FREE phone calls and text message to your friends. Kosmos messages can send photos, voice and video recordings and stickers to all of your friends and family – for FREE anywhere in the world.

Kosmos uses your wifi or phones data plan to make calls and send messages. Kosmos works on both iOS and Android devices – including iPad and Tablets. Kosmos lets users make calls to any landline and mobile phone numbers at very low rates – using Kosmos virtual currency.

Earn Kash And Koins Through Kosmos Offer Walls. You Can Use The Kosmos Koins To Make Phone Calls To Landline And Mobile Phone Numbers. Or Use The Koins To Buy Stickers From The Kosmos Shop.
