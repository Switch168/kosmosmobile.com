Support Information

If you have questions or feedback, feel free to contact us by sending a email to support@kosmosmobile.com

What is Kosmos and why should I use it?
Kosmos is a "Free" talk and text application that connects friends over WiFi or 3G. Kosmos users can call or message each other as much as they want – for Free. Kosmos users can also call out to any mobile or landline phone – with Kosmos Out at very low rates.
* When you use Kosmos on a 3G network you may be charged by your carrier
Does it cost anything to use Kosmos?
Kosmos to Kosmos calls and messages are always free.
Does Kosmos work on my phone?
Kosmos works on most iPhone and Android devices.
What is Kosmos Out?
Kosmos Out lets users make calls at very low rates from their smartphones to landline or mobile phone numbers all over the world.
What are Kash and Koins ?
Kosmos Kash and Koins are virtual currency that you can earn from within the app to make calls or purchases from shop.
What is Kosmos Offerwall ?
Kosmos gives our users access to 2 offerwalls – TapJoy and Supersonic. Where users can watch videos, download apps and take surveys to earn Kash. They can then convert the Kash to Koins to make international calls or buy stickers from Kosmos shop.
How do I get started with Kosmos?
Once you've downloaded Kosmos, you will receive an access code via SMS or callback to activate Kosmos. The access code ensures that you are the real owner of the cellphone number you have registered and prevents others from obtaining your access code. You can make calls and send texts via WiFi and 3G.
How do I find my friends ?
Kosmos will help you find you friends automatically – every time a new Kosmos User registers. We will notify you if that user is a contact of yours on your address book as a "suggested friend" in your "friends" tab. You will receive a push notification – telling you someone from your contacts has joined Kosmos.
What is a Kosmos ID ?
Kosmos ID allows users on the Kosmos network to find you. If you enable "search by Kosmos ID" in you profile and give yourself a unique ID – then any Kosmos user can search and add you as a friend. Even if you are not a contact in their address book.
Can I buy Koins in Kosmos ?
Yes – you can buy Koins with in-app purchase within Kosmos.
What is "send Koins to friends" ?
You can send Koins to your Kosmos friends anywhere in the world. They can use the Koins to make calls or buy stickers.
What are points and how do I get them ?
Kosmos points are earned for every minute of call you make via Kosmos Out.
What is in your "shop" ?
Kosmos shop has stickers that users can download and send to their friends. There are currently 5 stickers that are available to download for "free".
How do I tell my friends about Kosmos ?
Go to Kosmos account page – "tell-a-friend" tab and start inviting your friends via SMS, email or social media.
How do I report a problem with Kosmos ?
You can contact us via the "contact us" tab under accounts page. Tell us about the problem/issue and we will try to fix it as soon as possible.
How do I check to see how much you charge for calling landline or mobile phones ?
Under the "accounts" tab – we have our Kosmos Out Rate page. You can go into that page and look up all the countries and their per minute charge.
How can I tell how much the Kosmos Out call was ?
You can go into Koins tab under account page – and you can see your Koins history. It will show you the day, time, length and how many koins it cost.
How do I know how much Kash I have earned ?
In the "Koins" tab – you can see a running total of "Kash". You can also see more detailed information under "Kash History".
How do I make a Kosmos Out call ?
First make sure you have some Koins – then go to the "calls" tab – and press on the "call with Kosmos Out". This will take you to the Kosmos Out dialer. Select the country you want to call and enter the local phone number. You do not have to worry about any international prefixes – like "00" or "011" – we take care of that for you.
